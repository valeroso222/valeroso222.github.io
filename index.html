About Me

Yuhu Liu is a master student of Intelligent Systems and Informatics Laboratory in The University of Tokyo in Japan. He received his bachelor degree in Engineering from The University of Tokyo.His research areas are Human Computer Interaction (HCI), Virtual Reality (VR) and Haptics. He is interested in designing human perception by combining techniques of engineering and psychology.
